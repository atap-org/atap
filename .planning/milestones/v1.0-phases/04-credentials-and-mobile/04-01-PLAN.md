Build the W3C Verifiable Credentials infrastructure: database schema, credential issuance engine (trustbloc/vc-go), trust level derivation, Bitstring Status List revocation, SD-JWT selective disclosure, and per-entity AES-256-GCM encryption at rest.

Purpose: This is the foundation for trust enrichment. Without credentials, entities remain at trust level L0 and approvals carry no verifiable identity backing.
